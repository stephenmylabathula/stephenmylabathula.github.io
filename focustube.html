FOCUS-TUBE
FocusTube was first invented by myself and three other at PennApps Winter 2017 in Philadelphia, PA. All four of us were passionate about one thing - Online Learning. However, we hated how long just watching one online lecture or course could take. We also knew that watching videos at 2x speed didn't really help, because most people end up rewinding several portions or missing important points. Thus we created FocusTube. FocusTube uses computer vision to determine how attentive a user is and automatically changes video playback parameters like speed and volume to maximize the user's learning. It simply allows you to learn more in less time.
